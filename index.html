Hot Latte$6.50

A latte is a coffee drink with espresso, steamed milk and a layer of foam on top. What’s the difference between a latte vs a cappuccino? A cappuccino has equal parts espresso, steamed milk and foam (⅓ each). A latte has ⅓ espresso and 2/3 steamed milk, with a thin layer of foam on top. Oh, and a macchiato is just espresso with a thicker layer of foam on top..

How we began

We started in 2015 in a simple coffee shop, then we developed into a place. By 2019, we designed a very wonderful cafe, then the second floor was built in 2020. The place was designed at the convenience of customers, and everyone knows the place because of the distinctive location on the seashore and the very beautiful sand that makes people comfortable. Then the first floor was modified, and the kitchen and reception area were renovated in 2021, making it the most famous place in the city.
